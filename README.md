# AI-Powered Lead Nurturing Pipeline

## The Business Problem
Manual lead follow up is slow, inconsistent and doesn't scale. 
This automation ensures every lead gets a personalised AI-generated 
follow up email instantly and automatically.

## What It Does
1. Lead submits a HubSpot form
2. Make.com catches the submission instantly
3. OpenAI generates a personalised follow up email based on their 
   name, company and job title
4. Gmail sends the email automatically
5. Contact is created in HubSpot CRM
6. Slack notification fires with lead summary
7. Lead is logged to Google Sheets with timestamp

## Tools Used
- HubSpot — form capture and CRM
- Make.com — automation engine
- OpenAI GPT-3.5 — personalised email generation
- Gmail — email delivery
- Slack — team notifications
- Google Sheets — lead logging and reporting

## Business Value
- Zero manual follow up required
- Every lead gets a response in seconds not hours
- Personalised emails at scale
- Full lead tracking and reporting automatically
